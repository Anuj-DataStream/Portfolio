About Me

// engineer by nature

I'm Anuj Kumar, an aspiring engineering systems builder focused on creating practical, real-world solutions using IoT, automation, and web technologies.

Instead of just learning concepts, I focus on building working systems — combining hardware and software to solve real problems. My work includes sensor-based automation, system integration, and creating functional prototypes under real constraints.

One of my key projects includes rebuilding and optimizing a complex Arduino-based system under a strict deadline, restoring functionality and achieving recognition at a zonal science exhibition.

Currently, I am working on advancing my projects into more scalable and intelligent systems, while also exploring how technology can be applied in practical and business-oriented ways.

I aim to grow as an engineer who can not only build systems, but also think, adapt, and execute effectively in real-world scenarios.
